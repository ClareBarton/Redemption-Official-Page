HOW TO SURVIVE A NUKE

Surviving a nuclear explosion requires immediate action and proper preparation. Follow these steps to increase your chances of survival.

1. Preparation

  • Identify potential nuclear targets and move away from them if possible.
  • Stock up on emergency supplies: water, food, first aid, and protective gear.
  • Find or create a fallout shelter: basements or underground bunkers offer the best protection.

2. During the Explosion

  • If you see a bright flash, drop to the ground immediately and cover your head.
  • Stay behind thick walls or underground to reduce radiation exposure.
  • Do not look at the blast; it can cause permanent blindness.

3. Aftermath

  • Stay inside for at least 24-48 hours to avoid fallout exposure.
  • Use a radio to stay updated on emergency broadcasts.
  • Decontaminate by removing clothing and washing exposed skin thoroughly.
